Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in industrial AI/ML projects including computer vision systems (object detection, tracking, OCR, face recognition), smart surveillance, manufacturing quality control, logistics optimization, and edge AI deployment. I also collaborate on academic research in Medical AI and Federated Learning.

Do you work remotely or require on-site presence?

I prefer remote work but am available for on-site deployment, installation, and client meetings when necessary. I have experience deploying systems across 17+ locations and 150+ cameras with on-site coordination.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A typical computer vision system takes 1-3 months from concept to deployment. Data annotation projects can be delivered within 2-4 weeks. Custom deep learning models typically require 2-4 months including training and optimization.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes, I offer ongoing support, maintenance, model retraining, and system optimization services. Support packages can be customized based on your needs.

Can you help with PhD research or academic collaborations?

Absolutely! I'm actively interested in PhD opportunities and research collaborations, particularly in Medical AI, Computer Vision, Federated Learning, and Explainable AI. I have 9+ publications in journals and international conferences.

What hardware/software stack do you work with?

Software: Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, OpenCV, YOLO, scikit-learn, Flask. Hardware: Jetson Orin Nano, Jetson Xavier, NVIDIA GPUs, IP cameras, RTSP streams. I work with both cloud and edge deployment scenarios.

How do you handle data privacy and security?

I follow industry best practices for data privacy and security, including secure data handling, encryption, compliance with regulations (GDPR when applicable), and can work with on-premise solutions when cloud storage is not suitable.

What is your pricing structure?

Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, timeline, and deliverables. I offer hourly rates, fixed project fees, and retainer arrangements. Contact me with your project details for a customized quote.
